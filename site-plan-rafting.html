Harper's Ferry Rafting Site Plan

Matthew Mecham

WDD 130 - Section 4

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site plan is to create a comprehensive digital platform that effectively markets Harper's Ferry Rafting in Virginia. This platform aims to showcase the unique adventure experiences offered, promote outdoor activities along the picturesque Potomac and Shenandoah Rivers, and increase bookings through an engaging and user-friendly website. By highlighting the area's natural beauty, rich history, and diverse outdoor activities, the site will serve as a central hub for tourists and locals seeking thrilling water adventures. Additionally, it will provide essential information about rafting tours, safety guidelines, booking options, and customer testimonials to build trust and excitement among potential visitors.

Audience

The primary audience for Harper's Ferry Rafting in Virginia includes adventure seekers, nature enthusiasts, and families looking for outdoor recreational activities. This diverse group ranges from local residents to tourists visiting the historic region. It encompasses both experienced rafters and beginners seeking guided tours. Additionally, the audience includes educational groups, corporate teams interested in team-building exercises, and organizations planning group outings. The site will cater to individuals and groups looking for thrilling, memorable experiences while emphasizing safety, environmental stewardship, and the unique historical and geographical context of Harper's Ferry.
